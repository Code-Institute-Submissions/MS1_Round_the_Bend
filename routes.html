Belgium

Off to the races

Berlaar - Berlaar

244km

This ride takes you from the province of Antwerp through the provinces of Flemish Brabant, Walloon Brabant and back to the starting point in Berlaar. The route goes through a well-balanced mix of scenery and features both curvy backroads and straight main roads (no offroading).

Scotland

Getting from N to G

Nairn - Grantown-on-Spey

38km

Granted this is not so much a ride as it is a single road, but it's a biker's road. It has long corners, tight corners, rises, falls, straights, fantastic views, clear vision, awesome road surface and next to no traffic. If you're in the area go ahead and take this detour.

Around Loch Ness

Drumnadrochit - Drumnadrochit

102km

This route hugs the banks of Loch Ness starting in the center of Drumnadrochit. It goes past Urquhart Castle to Fort Augustus and from there it curves along the southern tip of the loch and on up the east bank toward Inverness. Eventhough there isn't a constant view of the loch, the scenery is beautiful and the road is a biker's dream.

Over the mountain

Kenmore - Amulree

19km

Starting at an intersection just outside Kenmore, this ride follows an unnamed road over the mountain to Amulree. With hairpin turns on both sides of the mountain and stunning views in between on the top we know you you'll want to turn right around and do it again.

The Netherlands

11 Frisian Cities

Leeuwarden - Leeuwarden

253km

This ride takes you along the route of the legendary Elfstedentocht. It's originally a day-long speed skating event on natural ice which takes in the 11 historical cities in the province of Friesland. This biker friendly version takes you along very scenic roads, all paved but some are very narrow.

Across the Veluwe

Harderwijk - Harderwijk

122km

The Veluwe is a national park in the center of the Netherlands which features woodlands, sand-drifts and heaths. Tourist attractions include the Kröller-Möller museum and former royal palace Het Loo.
