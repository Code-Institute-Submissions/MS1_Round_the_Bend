About us

We are a group of friends who enjoy getting together for a ride. Some have been riding motorcycles for over a decade, but our group includes all skill levels from complete novices to experienced bikers. We are based in Belgium, so most of our routes are also situated there, but at least once a year we plan a weeklong trip to another country.

Over the years we've racked up a lot of kilometers and accumulated some outstanding routes. After spending time putting them together and enjoying the ride, it just always seemed such a waste not to be able to share them with more people. Now that gpx files are finally supported by most SatNav manufacturers, we feel this is the best way to share them with you.
